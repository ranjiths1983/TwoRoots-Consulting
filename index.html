About TwoRoots

Two founders, two backgrounds, one shared commitment.

TwoRoots Consulting is a boutique consulting firm founded by two healthcare technology veterans with deep experience inside the payer and insurance industry. Our name reflects who we are — two founders, two backgrounds, one shared commitment to delivering results. We bring insider knowledge, technical expertise, and a passion for making transformation real, not just recommended. Our combined backgrounds in product strategy, Salesforce delivery, and technical quality management give our clients something the big firms can't offer: personalized, senior-level expertise from people who have actually done this work.

How We Help

Every engagement starts with listening.

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. We start by understanding where you are, where you want to go, and what's standing in the way.

01 — Assessment
Salesforce Platform Assessment
Starting at $3,500

Not sure if your Salesforce platform is working as hard as it should be? We take a deep look at how your organization currently uses Salesforce, identify gaps and underutilized capabilities, and deliver a clear, prioritized roadmap for improvement.

  • A comprehensive review of your current Salesforce setup
  • Identification of inefficiencies, gaps, and missed opportunities
  • A prioritized roadmap with actionable next steps
  • A 60-minute executive readout with our findings and recommendations
Best for: organizations that feel their Salesforce investment is underperforming, or want an outside perspective before further investment.
02 — Optimization
Process & Experience Optimization
$8,000 – $15,000

Great technology is only as good as the processes behind it. We analyze your end-to-end workflows, member and customer experiences, and operational processes to identify where friction lives and how to eliminate it.

  • End-to-end process mapping and current state analysis
  • Identification of pain points across member, provider, and operational workflows
  • Redesigned process recommendations aligned to your Salesforce capabilities
  • Implementation support and change management guidance
Best for: organizations navigating digital transformation, platform migrations, or struggling with adoption and operational efficiency.

Strategic Transformation Advisory
